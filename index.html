What We Do

Our Services

Three essential services that keep your asphalt protected, looking better, and lasting longer.

Pothole Repair

Potholes grow quickly as water and traffic break down the surface and underlying base. We repair each area using advanced cold mix, with typical job times from 2 hours to 4-5 hours depending on size and depth. The result is a durable, safe surface with a repair life of around 10-15 years.

Crack Sealing

Unsealed cracks allow water into the pavement, which weakens the basecourse and leads to potholes. We seal cracks and joints using Promix Asphalt Cracksealer, a professional-grade bitumen-based product designed for lasting protection. The result is long-term crack control, with service life significantly extended when paired with Asphalt Seal Coating.

Asphalt Seal Coating

A fresh seal coat protects your asphalt, improves its appearance, and helps extend pavement life. We apply Promix SuperSeal, a bitumen emulsion with polymers and mineral fillers that protects against tyre abrasion, UV, rain, and mild chemicals, while maintaining a deep black finish. One coat typically lasts 5-7 years, and a second coat extends life further.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to what people ask us before they book.

Is asphalt sealing waterproof?

Yes. Promix SuperSeal forms a protective barrier that keeps water from penetrating the asphalt surface. This is exactly what prevents moisture from reaching the basecourse — the main cause of cracks and potholes over time.

How long will the work last?

Pothole repairs can last 10-15 years if the surrounding asphalt stays firm, no alligator cracking develops nearby, and water doesn't pool over the repaired area. Seal coating typically lasts 5-7 years, and a second coat extends life further. Crack sealing on its own is long-lasting, and significantly extended when topped with seal coating.

How much does it cost?

Cost depends on the scope — driveway size, condition, and which services are needed. We provide free, no-obligation assessments and quotes within 24 hours of visiting your site. For larger jobs over $1,200, a 35% deposit may be required before work begins.

When can I park after asphalt seal coating?

You can drive and park on the surface after 48 hours. For the first 7-9 days, please avoid sharp stationary turns and quick acceleration or braking. Full curing takes about 2 weeks.

When can I drive after a pothole or crack repair?

About 1 hour after the work is finished — no restrictions after that.

What does the process look like from quote to completion?

After your enquiry, we visit your site within 1-2 days and email a quote within 24 hours. Once approved, work is typically scheduled within a week, weather permitting (asphalt sealing requires at least 2 consecutive dry days).

Do you do small driveways, or only large jobs?

Both. We work on residential driveways and small to mid-size commercial carparks. There's no minimum job size for crack sealing or pothole repair — we'll fix whatever needs fixing.

How does payment work?

Residential customers pay on completion. For commercial clients, we accept the same arrangement, and we're also comfortable with standard 20-30 day invoice terms. For jobs over $1,200, we ask for a 35% deposit upfront.

What does the 2-year workmanship guarantee cover?

If something fails within 2 years because of how we did the work, we return and fix it at no charge. The guarantee doesn't cover new damage caused by external factors — for example, heavy machinery on a residential driveway. The exact terms are included in every quote.
